  • Logged human response signals such as engagement, confusion, laughter, and trust-related cues.

Problem. Conversational AI systems often struggle to adapt to individual users over repeated interactions. Static prompts can produce inconsistent behavior, poor timing, and weak personalization.

Approach. This project uses a dual-LLM architecture where one model generates responses and a second model edits prompts based on interaction context, persona constraints, and observed user response signals. The system is designed for embodied social interaction, where timing, tone, and adaptation are critical.
